Help

Account Help


Reset Password

  • If you need to reset your password, just go to this link here.
  • If you can not remember your email address, contact us, and we can help you to look up your account.
  • If you can not remember any information, we can not help you.

Profile

  • If you are logged in, you can click on the profile link to see your profile.
  • Your profile is public, except for your email address, which is private.
  • If you want to edit your profile, you can click the edit profile link on the upper right hand corner of your profile.
  • Pictures that you upload will be automatically cropped.

Twooting Guide

  • You can create a new twoot after logging in, from your Home page.
  • A Twoot can only be 10 characters long, that is by design.
  • You can like and retwoot twoots. You can also comment on twoots.
  • If you retwoot a twoot it will only show up on you and your followers homepages.
  • If you make a twoot, and it gets popular, it can potentially show up on the Explore page under trending.
  • If you want to follow a user, click on their username, and follow them from their profile.
  • If you want to comment on a twoot, just click anywhere on the twoot to get to the comments page. Or click the comment button.
